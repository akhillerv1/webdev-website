Island Beach State Park

Address: Seaside Park, NJ 08752
One of the few undeveloped barrier island covering 3,000 acres. Activities include fishing, swimming, sunbathing and surfing. The state's largest osprey colony, as well as peregrine falcons, wading birds, shorebirds, waterfowl and migrating songbirds, are found here.

Liberty State Park

Address: Jersey City
If you want a great view of the Manhattan skyline and the Statue of Liberty without the crowds and craziness, you need to stop here. This would be a perfect picnic spot during warmer months!

Gateway National Recreation Area

Address: Fort Hancock, Sandy Hook, NJ
Site of the oldest lighthouse in America.

Pine Barrens' Wharton State Forest

Address: NJ
This unique environmental treasure covers approximately one million acres of undeveloped land in southern New Jersey, which has been designated as a biosphere reserve by the United Nations and a "Last Great Place" by the Nature Conservancy.

High Point State Park

Address: Sussex, NJ
This park is at the northern tip of New Jersey and the highest elevation in the state.It offers beautiful hiking trails.There are numerous picnic grounds-some of them with barbecue facilities.Lake Marcia has a beach and swimming facilities.The NJ Veterans Monument is at the highest point.The surrounding view areas offer lush,gorgeous ,unobstructed views of North/Northwest New Jersey, Eastern Pennsylvania and South New York State.
