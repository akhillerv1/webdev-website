Whale and Dolphin Watching Cruise from Cape May

Address: 1218 Wilson Dr, Cape May, NJ 08204, USA
Take a sightseeing cruise off the coast of Cape May to the best locations for spotting whales, dolphins, and porpoises. Your certified naturalist guide offers live commentary about the area’s ocean life and the animals’ natural habitat. The boat is fully equipped with wheelchair access, bathrooms, and a snack bar for your convenience.

Poltergeist Interactive Escape Room in Northfield, New Jersey

Address: 513 Tilton Rd, Northfield, NJ 08225, USA
With your team of friends and family, solve the puzzles to break out of this haunted house escape room. Book your tickets ahead of time to guarantee your time slot and guarantee against sold-out days and times. Take some time out from gambling or laying on the beach to put your critical thinking skills and teamwork to the test.

Sunset Safari

Address: 1212 Wilson Dr, Cape May, NJ 08204, USA
We get views of the wildlife of the salt marsh that people almost never have an opportunity to see. We sail daily from April until October so we always know where we are likely to see the most interesting birds and with 25 years of experience running these tours we know where we are likely to have the best trip. Our route varies daily depending on conditions.

New Jersey Lunch Cruise

Address: 1500 Harbor Blvd, NJ
When sunshine, good company and city skyline views combine, there’s no better place to enjoy an afternoon than aboard an outdoor lunch cruise. Departing from Lincoln Harbor Marina in Weehawken NJ, this is the perfect way to spend a relaxing afternoon on the water. Make your way to your outdoor private table. Indulge yourself in a delicious all-you-can-eat buffet freshly prepared on board while listening to top hits! Take in the stunning views of the iconic New York City skyline. Come aboard and spend your afternoon on this cruise!

Historical Walking Tour in Mount Holly

Address: Burlington County Prison Museum
We provide fun, informative, and entertaining guided historical walking tours of beautiful downtown Mount Holly, NJ. Come take a walk through the nooks and crannies of local history!
